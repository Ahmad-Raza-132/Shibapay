ABOUT SHIBABITCOIN

ShibaBitcoin is a pioneering cryptocurrency designed to bridge the gap between digital and real-world economies, making cryptocurrency practical and accessible for everyone. Built on the Binance Smart Chain (BSC) as a BEP-20 token, SHIBTC is optimised for seamless, secure, and low-cost transactions. Our goal is to make digital payments simple, user-friendly, and versatile, enabling SHIBTC to be used across various settings, from daily purchases and digital assets to global money transfers.

OUR MISSION

Our mission is to create a reliable, accessible cryptocurrency that enhances everyday payments by combining the efficiency of blockchain technology with real-world usability. ShibaBitcoin aims to be a universal payment solution, allowing users to transact with confidence, whether they’re making in-game purchases, sending money overseas, or buying NFTs. By fostering partnerships and integrating with familiar payment systems, ShibaBitcoin aims to support a world where digital currency becomes a seamless part of everyday life.

OUR values

At ShibaBitcoin, we envision a future where cryptocurrency is as widely accepted and accessible as traditional money. Our goal is to make SHIBTC a globally recognised currency that fits smoothly into people’s lives, supporting financial freedom, convenience, and flexibility. By expanding partnerships and focusing on real-world usability, ShibaBitcoin is setting a new standard for digital payments in the growing global economy.

SHIBABITCOIN CORE VALUES

Simple, secure, and scalable
